Holy Cow!

Building a kick-ass burger brand with a conscience.

Step 1.

What’s this brand about, dude ?

Holy Cow's mission is to create a world where people can enjoy a healthy and guilt-free meal. We want to be the most ecological fastfood-chain in the industry by spearheading innovation.

To achieve this we go full circle. We make use of the latest trends an innovations, like Clean-meat, farm-to-table and online services like TooGoodToGo. We focus on what matters, to insure the best quality and the lowest impact for our beloved planet.
