Jan Huysmans is a digital designer and artist based in Antwerp.

Hi, I'm Jan.

I'm a designer and artist working on a variety of projects, including the development of apps and websites.

I'm passionate about building brands, interfaces, or anything that sort of looks like a system really.

I use the Adobe Suite (Ps, Ai, Xd,..) or Figma on the daily, I’m also an avid user of Miro.

I have a keen interest in interior/product design and architecture. And I gained some experience in 3D-modelling with Solidworks and SketchUp in the past.

I love art too.

Usually my work is based on studies of rhythm, color or pattern, involving themes like repetition, modularity, or automation.

I’ve participated in group exhibitions in Brussels, Antwerp or Ghent, among others. My next goal is to explore Creative Coding as a new technique.
