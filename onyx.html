Onyx.

A handmade font that rocks.

Onyx is the first font I ever made.

Onyx isn't meant to be slick.

Getting the forms to be sort of legible but still sort of wonky was a challenge, but I really loved to lay down the puzzle.

I made some sketches by hand before developing the letters digitally in Illustrator and FontForge.
