Werkplekleren 2

During WPL 2 we had te opportunity to team up and do 4 sprint around a real-life case.

We created Buildr. a matchmaking app that matches our users with their dream jobs.
During these 4 sprints we did user research, brand development and we also protoyped the app and built the website for our product.

Buildr.

A job-matching app for construction, metal and logistics.

During WPL2 our team made an application for matching candidates with their dream jobs.
Buildr. is an all-in solution where people can build their profiles, match with potential employers or candidates, and connect trough a built-in chatbox.

Case Study - Buildr.
