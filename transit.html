TRANSIT

2018

In 2018 I designed 5 sculptures for ‘Transit’, a techno/house-event with headliners like Mall Grab.

The forms were designed in Cinema 4D, and edited in Rhino to a waffle-grid structure. The cardboard cut-outs measure about 2.4 meters and were made by a specialised firm in the Netherlands.
