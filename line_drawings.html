Line Drawings.

2015-2018

In the period 2015-2018 I made several series of drawings in ink, in this work I consistently use the inks from the Bic-brand. Each piece is built up out of hundreds of parallel horizontal lines.

COLOR STUDIES

Some of these works are constructed out of ‘rhythms’ of color, forming a new spectrum.

Limited Colours

2018

A set of 8 drawings made in Bic on A4 paper. Experiment with the 8 colors from the limited-edition colors of the brand.

The Four Seasons

After Jakob Smits | 2016-2017

Interpretation after the work of the painter/draftsman Jakob Smits. Depiction of four seasons in a dedicated Bic-color. Technique-study for line-drawings with figuration.

Autumn

After Jakob Smits | 2017

Colored version of 'Autumn'.

Color Study. Construction with horizontal lines in varying colors and length. Made for a group exhibit in the Jakob Smitsmuseum, Mol.

Visit the Jacob Smitsmuseum.

Rhythm-Spectrum

2017 | 270x180cm

Extensive color study with the Bic brand colors. 64 handmade A4-drawings consisting of 4 colors (Red, Green, Blue and Black). Show at group exhibit 'The shape to come' at Kunstencentrum België, Hasselt.

Dolls

2017

Another figurative piece. This work was made by commission of the current owner. The image is a re-creation of a photograph that got destroyed.
