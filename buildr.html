Buildr.

A job-matching app for construction, metal and logistics.

During WPL2 our team made an application for matching candidates with their dream jobs.
Buildr. is an all-in solution where people can build their profiles, match with potential employers or candidates, and connect trough a built-in chatbox.

The case.

Build a job-matching app and an accessible website for its users.

Develop the brand identity for brand X. Empower users to find work easily.

Research.

How does our audience experience looking for a job ?

What we've learned.

  • Age is an issue.
  • People have a hard time negotiating.
  • Not everybody is proud of their resume.
  • Hard to really get to know the company.
  • Lots of administration.
  • Difficult to have an overview.

Meet Jos.

He represents our ‘candidate’. A motivated guy who has a hard time finding a job.

Jos worries about his age a little bit, but he has a ton of experience.
