Collider.

2018-2022

The Collider-series represents a new chapter in my personal work.

I try to apply a systemic approach to each drawing, as I'm searching for patters, images. You can see some of these pieces below. Each drawing has been executed in ink. Some were executed by hand, others digitally, combining Adobe Illustrator and Inkscape with my Axidrax Plotter.

Collider A3

2021-2022

This set was made between 2021 and 2022. Each drawing is done in ink, 32x24cm on 220g Ca' Grain paper.

Collider Large

2020-2021

This set consists of larger pieces that were executed freehanded, over a period of 5 weeks per piece.

Pentel-ink, 80x60cm on Waterford 320g paper.

Digital

2021-2022

Digital pieces from the Collider-series.

These pieces are the result of digital experiments, combining multiple layers of patters and color.

Axidraw Plots

2022

Prints from the Collider-series.

This series started in 2022, I saved some cash and bought an Axidraw SE drawing machine. I digitized some of my work with Adobe Illustrator and Inkscape. I was aiming for a more economical approach to drawing, opposed to manual labour. This resulted in using robotics as a new creative tool.
